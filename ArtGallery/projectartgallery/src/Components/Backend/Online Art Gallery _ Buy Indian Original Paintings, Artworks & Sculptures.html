Masters Collection presents MyIndianArt.com

 

An Online Art Gallery on Modern & Contemporary Indian Art

 

Masters Collection is over Two Decades old Indian Art Gallery based in Kolkata, India. The primary vision of the gallery is to procure, advice & promote an exclusive range of Indian Contemporary Art, Indian Modern Art and all other genres of Indian Painting by exploring and evaluating artists from across India.

Over the last few years, Indian Art has gained immense fame not only on national grounds but also on an international level. And it is with the aim to continue the respect and charm, that this gallery currently showcases some of the finest collection of Indian Artworks & Indian Paintings of eminent and famous artists of Bengal & rest of India.

Taking Modern Indian Art To Higher Heights Of Recognition

Being professionally managed, the gallery has held several solo & group art shows by co-branding with several known corporates like Seagrams, Taj Bengal, ABN Amro Bank & HSBC who have the matching belief for art promotion as a part of their CSR.

Apart from the artworks of renowned and established artists, this gallery also provides a platform and promotion of artworks for young upcoming artists.

MyIndianArt.com - the official site owned by Master's Collection Art Gallery - displays a wide range of Indian Contemporary & Modern Art & Indian Paintings Online. Each piece of Indian Painting on the site is original Indian Art that is carefully reviewed and chosen by our team to be put on the site along with the artist's profile/resume. These exclusive pieces of artwork are sure to catch the eyes and appreciation of the discerning art collector or even a first-time art buyer.

The original paintings are of different mediums like – Oil Paintings, Acrylic Paintings, Watercolor Paintings & Charcoal Paintings.

MyIndianArt.com showcases artworks on various style & subjects like – Abstract Paintings, Landscape Paintings, Portrait Paintings, Still Life Paintings, Figurative Paintings and Mythological Paintings.

MyIndianArt.com has been made a very user-friendly site where the buyer can search for specific Indian Artist or do an advanced search based on Price, Size, Format or Style to shortlist Indian Original Paintings as per his/her choice. Buying Indian Art Online is easy from the site through a simple inquiry.

The site is updated on a regular basis by the gallery with information on new additions of Indian Art, Indian Paintings & Indian Artists being communicated to our esteemed art buyers & collectors on a periodic basis.

MyIndianArt App - the First App on Finest Indian Original Art - is now available to browse on Android & IOS Platform.

Purchase Art And Contribute To A Better World

As a responsibility to give back to the society, a portion of every sale that proceeds from this site goes towards Child Cancer Treatment at Netaji Cancer Research Institute (NCRI), Kolkata.

Therefore, by buying art from this site, you would automatically help in saving a child’s life.

Finally, we wish to thank you for visiting our site and hope you enjoy viewing the wide range of handpicked collection of these beautiful pieces of Indian Original Paintings and Indian Artworks. Please feel free to contact us with your inquiry.

Thank you for your patronage & we look forward to your support
